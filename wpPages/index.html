Who is Barb?

Great question!  So many answers!  I love my family, teaching (but, honestly my students  are my teachers), reading, solving problems (a.k.a fixing things), laughing (mostly at myself), traveling/camping and the simple things in life i.e. seeing fireflies on summer nights, the smell of a campfire, the taste of a perfectly ripe fruit/vegetable, the feel of moss under my toes, and hearing the cacophony of frogs.
